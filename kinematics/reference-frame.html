Notice that I have introduced the unit vectors \(\hat{b}_x\) and \(\hat{b}_y\) for the reference frame \(B\) but \(\hat{b}_z\) is not shown though it is orthogonal to both \(\hat{b}_x\) and \(\hat{b}_y\).
